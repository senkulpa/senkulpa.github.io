Senkulpa is a "hardcore anarchy" Minecraft server. Gameplay enhancement plugins pointed towards making the game harder are in place. No gameplay changing item/power can be bought with money - all players are equal (admins are gods).

The word "senkulpa" should mean something like lawlessness in esperanto, not sure.

Important info

  • Server: mc.senkulpa.org
  • IRC: #senkulpa on IRCnet (open.ircnet.net, webchat)
  • I recommend this open source launcher (it's in English)
